  2. Enter your registered email address

  4. Click the "Login" button

  1. Login to your account
  2. Click on "My Passes" in the dashboard
  3. Select the pass you want to view
  4. You can:
    • View pass details
    • Download pass as PDF
    • View QR code
    • Check validity period
Note: Make sure to keep your pass details secure and don't share them with others.

  1. Open the pass on your mobile device
  2. Show the QR code to the conductor
  3. The conductor will scan the QR code using their device
  4. Wait for verification
Important:
  • Make sure your pass is valid before boarding
  • Keep your phone charged
  • Have a backup screenshot of your pass

  1. Login to your account
  2. Go to "My Passes"
  3. Select the pass you want to renew
  4. Click on "Renew Pass"
  5. Choose the renewal period
  6. Make the payment
  7. Download your renewed pass
Note:
  • You can renew your pass up to 7 days before expiry
  • Payment must be completed for renewal to be processed
  • You'll receive a confirmation email after successful renewal
